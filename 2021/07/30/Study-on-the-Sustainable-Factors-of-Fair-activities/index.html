Study on the Sustainable Factors of Fair activities

General introduction

This is a project aiming to figure out the sustainability factors of fair activities nowadays and our study team needs to finish an essay about our study results at the end of the project.

Brief preview about fair activities

In this stage, we focus on study different fair activities and markets all over the world and analyse their common features.

Fair activities are markets with special themes, they are more flexible than markets. There are some famous fairs still active in cities.

  1. Indian moonlight Market (started from 1650)

  2. Anpawa market, Bangkok, Thailand

  3. Camden market, UK(started from1974)

    it is famous among teenagers because it has rock and punk style.

Many fair activities can not be held because they are non-profit, temporary and having no management organizations.

The main purpose of this project is to find the reasons and nature of the sustainable development of fair activities.

Research Contents

We decide to do the research and analyse fairs all over the world following these contents:

  1. Time period of fairs

  2. The theme and special features of fairs

  3. The scale of fairs

  4. The organizational form of fairs (Whether they are run by the government or by private companies)

  5. The way of earning profits of fairs

  6. The improvements fairs bring to this city (e.g. Stimulate consumption and tourism)

  7. The sustainable factors of fairs (This will be included in the questionnaire)

    1. Analysis of development factors

    2. The creation of index system according to the analysis results

    3. Make the questionnaire about sustainable factors of fairs
